About AniTrack

AniTrack is a legal anime discovery site built to help people find trending, seasonal, and top-rated anime quickly. It uses public anime metadata to make browsing simple on mobile and desktop.

What the site does

  • Shows trending anime and seasonal picks.
  • Displays official trailers when available.
  • Links users to legal streaming and watch options.
  • Helps users search and compare anime faster.

Our goal

The goal is to build a useful, fast, mobile-friendly anime hub with real value for fans. The long-term plan is to grow through SEO, repeat visitors, and legal monetization methods such as ads, affiliate links, and sponsorships.

Data sources

Anime information is sourced from Jikan, the unofficial MyAnimeList API, and trailers are shown only when available through official public sources.
